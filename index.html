Nickname Timer

Uses your discord server specific nickname to set a timer. Can be useful when you want to tell your friends when you will return.

Learn more

VC Stats

The bot collects and summarizes your and your discord server members voice channel acitivity. There is also a global voice channel activity top list where you can compare your and your discord members activity to other discord servers.

Learn more

Voice Activity Roles

You can activate the voice activity roles function. Based on how active you or your server members are in voice chats, the bot rewards you and your discord members with custom roles that you've set up.
